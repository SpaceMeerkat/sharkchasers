About Shark Chasers

Join me on a thrilling global quest to encounter and document the incredible diversity of sharks in their natural habitats. This isn't just a project; it's my personal journey, driven by a passion for exploration and a fascination with the wonders beneath the waves.

At Shark Chasers, every dive is an adrenaline-fueled exploration into the unknown. Armed with a list of 30 shark species from the 2001 Sharks edition of Top Trumps, I traverse the globe, seeking out these majestic creatures and sharing the sheer joy and excitement of the underwater world.

This project is more than just ticking off a checklist; it's a personal odyssey fueled by curiosity and the desire to experience the extraordinary. From the silent depths to the vibrant coral reefs, each dive is a chapter in the ongoing adventure of Shark Chasers.

The Proof is in the Pudding

Immerse yourself in the heart-pounding moments as I come face to face with sharks from around the world. The videos diaries shown in this site show not only the raw beauty of these creatures but also the thrill of the chase and the unique environments they call home.
